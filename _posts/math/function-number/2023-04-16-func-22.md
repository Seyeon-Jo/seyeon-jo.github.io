>

> S<sub>n</sub> =
> $$ {n} \over {2} $$

> $$ {n} \over {2} $$

> $$ {t\_{n+1}} \over {t_n} $$
>
> t<sub>n</sub> = a r<sup>n - 1</sup>

> S<sub>n</sub> =
> $$ {a(r^n - 1)} \over {r - 1} $$
