<u>방법 1</u>

```
with open('pi_digits.txt', 'w') as file:
file.write('3.14')

with open('pi_digits.txt', 'r') as file:
line = file.readline()
print(line)

with open('pi_digits.txt', 'a') as file:
file.write('\n1234')

with open('pi_digits.txt', 'r') as file:
```
